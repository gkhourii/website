Leim is the Irish dance club at the University of Michigan. For three years I have been performing guitar as part of the live music accompinament.
Each year the ensemble has grown in size; this year we had an accordion, violin, double bass, and acoustic guitar. The Scarborough Set was our special live music feature during the annual recital.
